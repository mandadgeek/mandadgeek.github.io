About Chris:

@thewhoadyman

What could I tell you that would give you a little insight into who I am and why I am who I am? I'll try and keep it simple for you:

  • - I'm Filipino American, born in Houston, Texas and no, I have not learned how to speak Tagalog

  • - I'm married to my best friend and we are the proud parents of a Sharbrador named Kikko and a Corgi named Korree

  • - I used to breakdance in high school and that's when I fell in love with old school hip-hop and dance music

  • - I've messed around as a DJ (house and UK garage when it was still a thing), nothing serious, but occassionaly still make house mixes

  • - I was part of a Catholic youth group for half a dozen years

  • - I'm a Team Beachbody Coach for health, fitness, and leadership development but have been dormant for a few years

  • - I love video games and have gamed since owning an Intellivision when I was 5

  • - I played Dungeons and Dragons (and similar pen and paper RPGs) consistently every other week for about 3-4 years

There's more to know and I'm sure you'll see much more here at NRDPLS. We're about showing people that we are all more than just the first blush, so there's a quick snapshot of some of the stuff that makes me, well, me.
