About Tim:

@yaboyytim

I'm not good at writing a profile about myself, so here's an easy bullet-point list of some facts about me :D


  • * I was born and raised for most of my childhood in German (I was also born with a video game controller in my hands, lol)


  • * I'm a military brat (GO ARMY!) and have been lucky enough to have traveled and lived around the world


  • * And because of the military lifestyle, I absolutely love traveling, airports, train stations, and road trips. You can't get me on a boat, though, unless you want vomit all over you.


  • * I love music, but who doesn't, right? I'd say my music taste is pretty eclectic; if I like the sound moves me in any way, it's good to me!


  • * I love and play all kinds of sports, but mainly basketball. It's my first love <3


  • * Snowboarding is my biggest addiction and has been since I first learned in 2007. The most I've gone in one season was 12 times XD


  • * It's weird to say, but I'm an outdoorsy homebody, lol. By that, I mean I love staying in at home most of the time, but I also love doing anything outdoors such as (but not limited to) biking, fishing, camping, hiking, etc.


  • * As a homebody, I'm all about playing video games, reading, watching movies, shows and anime. That's all I ever do and want to do on weekends.


  • * My main passion and career is Photography. I studied and graduated as a professional photographer at Hallmark Institute of Photography. That was one of the best years of my life, hands down. Need a portrait or product shot taken? Holla at ya boyyyy. (No weddings, pl0x) You can visit my website to check some of my work out: www.timtolentino.com :)


  • * I am a crazy dog person. I love, love, LOVE man's best friend! There has never been a time in my life where my family hasn't owned a dog. Due to being a military brat, we've had to give away most of them when moving, sadly. However, I'm a proud father of a welsh corgi named Milo and he is my whole world <3


  • * Lastly, in the words of Jason Mraz, "I'm known to keep diggin' on existence." Simple as that!


I think I covered a good amount, but I'm sure you'll get to know more about me as the posts come and go, and I hope I get to know about you all as well! Stay nerdy ::Spockhandsign::
