Every month I try to put together a new playlist on Soundcloud to give you a sense of the kind of music that moves me. You can always find them on my profile page here. This month, Feb. 2016, I'm feeling the disco vibe like never before. I would also like to make this a small tribute to the recently deceased Maurice White, co-founder of the timeless seminal band Earth, Wind and Fire, one of my all-time favorite bands. RIP Maurice, and thank you for the band and the music that shaped my childhood.

My monthly playlists are living playlists so I will continuall update them throughout the month with new songs that fit my fancy. You can also check out all of my older playlists on Soundcloud as well.
